How to Play Word Scramble

  1. Look at the jumbled letters on the screen.
  2. Try to unscramble the letters to make a word in Te Reo Māori.
  3. Type your answer in the input box.
  4. Click the "Submit" button to check if your answer is correct.
  5. If your answer is right, you'll see a "Correct!" message, and a new scrambled word will appear.
  6. If your answer is wrong, you'll see an "Incorrect. Try again." message, and you can try again.

Have fun unscrambling the words and learning Te Reo Māori!
