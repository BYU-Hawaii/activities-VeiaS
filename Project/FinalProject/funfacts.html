Lion's are the king of the jungle but they don't actually live in the jungle. Watch this video to learn more about Lion's

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They live in Africa and Asia. Elephants use their long trunks to grab food, drink water, and even take baths! They love to eat plants and can remember their friends for many years.

Monkeys are playful animals that live in trees in many warm parts of the world. Some monkeys have tails that can grab onto branches like an extra hand! They love to eat fruits and nuts, and they're very good at solving puzzles.

Penguins are birds that can't fly, but they're excellent swimmers! They live in cold places like Antarctica. Penguins have special feathers that keep them warm and dry in icy water.They love to eat fish and often slide on their bellies across the ice.

Chickens are farm birds that you might see in a barnyard. Did you know that chickens are related to dinosaurs? They like to eat seeds, insects, and worms. Chickens can remember over 100 different faces of people or animals!

Octopuses are amazing sea creatures with eight arms called tentacles. Octopuses can change their color and shape to blend in with their surroundings! They're very smart and can solve puzzles to get their favorite foods like crabs and fish.
