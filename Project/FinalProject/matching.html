How to Play Word Matching

  1. Look at the images and words on the screen.
  2. Click on a word and then click on the matching image.
  3. If you make a correct match, you'll see a "Correct!" message.
  4. If you make an incorrect match, you'll see an "Incorrect. Try again." message.
