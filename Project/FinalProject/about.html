About Te Reo Playground

Te Reo Playground is an interactive website designed to help children learn Te Reo Maori, the language of the Cook Islands, in a fun and engaging way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the preservation and appreciation of the Te Reo Maori language among younger generations. We believe that language is a vital part of cultural identity, and by making the learning process enjoyable, we can inspire children to embrace and celebrate their heritage.

Our Games and Activities

Te Reo Playground offers a variety of interactive games and activities to cater to different learning styles and preferences. From word scrambles and matching games to short stories, our content is designed to be both educational and entertaining.

Word Scramble

Test your Te Reo Maori vocabulary by unscrambling letters to form words. This game challenges your problem-solving skills while reinforcing your understanding of Maori words.

Matching Game

Match Maori words with their corresponding images to improve your visual association and recognition skills. This game is perfect for visual learners.

Short Stories

Immerse yourself in the world of Te Reo Maori by reading simple stories designed specifically for language learners. These stories provide context and help you develop a better understanding of the language's structure and flow.

Get Involved

We welcome feedback and suggestions from our users. If you have ideas for improving our website or would like to contribute new content, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

Join us on this journey of learning and preserving Te Reo Maori, one game and story at a time.
