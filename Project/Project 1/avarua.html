Avarua - The Heart of Rarotonga

Nestled along the north coast of Rarotonga, Avarua is the vibrant hub of the Cook Islands. This bustling town is where the island's rich culture and modern amenities converge, offering visitors a unique blend of traditional and contemporary experiences.

Stroll through the main streets of Avarua, where you'll find colorful markets, local crafts shops, and lively eateries serving up delicious island cuisine. Don't miss the opportunity to visit the iconic Punanga Nui Cultural Market, where you can immerse yourself in the local arts, music, and dance.

Avarua is also home to several historical sites, including the Cook Islands National Museum and the CICC Church, which offer insights into the island's rich history and cultural heritage.
