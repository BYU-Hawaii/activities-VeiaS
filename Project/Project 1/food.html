Savoring the Flavors of Rarotonga

Rarotonga's unique cuisine is a delightful fusion of Polynesian and European influences, showcasing the island's abundant fresh produce and seafood. From traditional dishes to modern interpretations, the flavors of Rarotonga are sure to tantalize your taste buds.

One of the island's most iconic dishes is the Ika Mata, a raw fish dish cured in citrus juices and coconut cream. This refreshing and flavorful delicacy is a staple in Rarotonga and a must-try for seafood lovers.

For a truly authentic experience, indulge in a traditional Umukai feast. This ancient cooking method involves slow-roasting meats, vegetables, and starches in an earth oven, resulting in tender and flavorful dishes infused with a smoky aroma.

While Rarotonga proudly celebrates its culinary heritage, the island has also embraced modern dining trends. Don't miss the opportunity to try a delicious Poke Bowl, a refreshing and healthy dish featuring marinated raw fish, vegetables, and rice, often with a unique Polynesian twist.
