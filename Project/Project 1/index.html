A Photo Tour of Cook Island

Popular Islands in the Cook Islands


Welcome to our virtual journey through the breathtaking Cook Islands, a tropical paradise in the heart of the South Pacific. This website serves as a captivating photo tour, immersing you in the natural beauty and rich culture of these idyllic islands. Explore the pristine beaches, turquoise lagoons, and lush landscapes of Rarotonga, the vibrant main island. Discover the serene tranquility of Aitutaki, renowned for its picture-perfect atolls and crystal-clear waters. Venture to the remote and untouched islands of Manihiki and Mangaia, where traditional lifestyles harmoniously coexist with nature. Along the way, savor the unique flavors of authentic Cook Island cuisine, celebrating the fusion of Polynesian and European influences in every bite.

Start this journey by knowing and understanding a little more about the unique islands of:

  • Rarotonga
  • Aitutaki
  • Mangaiia
  • Manihiki
