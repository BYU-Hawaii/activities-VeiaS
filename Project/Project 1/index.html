A Photo Tour of Cook Island

Popular Islands in the Cook Islands


Welcome to our virtual journey through the captivating island of Rarotonga, the heart of the Cook Islands. This website serves as a captivating photo tour, immersing you in the natural beauty and rich culture of this idyllic paradise. Explore the pristine beaches, towering mountains, and vibrant town of Avarua, where traditional Cook Islands life blends seamlessly with modern amenities.

Explore the Highlights of Rarotonga

  • Avarua: The bustling town center and main hub of Rarotonga, where you can experience the island's vibrant culture and local life.
  • Beaches: Discover the stunning white-sand beaches and crystal-clear lagoons that have made Rarotonga a world-renowned tropical destination.
  • Mountains: Venture into the lush interior of the island and hike through the breathtaking mountain landscapes, offering panoramic views and opportunities for adventure.
  • Food: Savor the unique flavors of authentic Cook Island cuisine, a delightful fusion of Polynesian and European influences, showcasing the island's abundant fresh produce and seafood.
