Rarotonga's Stunning Beaches

Rarotonga is renowned for its breathtaking beaches, where the turquoise waters of the South Pacific meet pristine white sands and swaying palm trees. These idyllic shores are a true paradise for beach lovers and water enthusiasts alike.

One of the island's most popular beaches is Muri Beach, a picturesque lagoon framed by lush palm trees and surrounded by a vibrant coral reef. This beach offers excellent snorkeling opportunities, where you can explore the underwater world teeming with colorful marine life.

For those seeking a more secluded experience, the beaches of Titikaveka and Aroa offer a tranquil escape. These hidden gems are perfect for relaxing, sunbathing, and taking in the stunning natural beauty of Rarotonga.
