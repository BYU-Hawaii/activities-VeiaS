Exploring Rarotonga's Mountains

Avarua Beach Mountain Food

Rarotonga's rugged interior is dominated by lush mountain ranges, offering breathtaking vistas and endless opportunities for adventure. From challenging hikes to scenic drives, the island's mountainous landscapes are a true feast for the senses.

For those seeking an unforgettable hiking experience, the Cross Island Trek is a must-do. This challenging yet rewarding trail takes you through dense rainforests, past cascading waterfalls, and to the highest point of the island, offering panoramic views that will leave you in awe.

If you prefer a more leisurely experience, the Raemaru Road offers a scenic drive through the island's lush interior, with opportunities to stop and explore hidden gems like the Wigmore's Waterfall and the ancient Ara Metua road.
