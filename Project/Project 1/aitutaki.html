Kia orana! I'm Tavai, and while Rarotonga will always be my home, my heart belongs to the breathtaking island of Aitutaki, often referred to as the "Jewel of the Pacific." This tiny island paradise is a true natural wonder, with its stunning turquoise lagoon and pristine beaches.

One of the best ways to experience Aitutaki's beauty is by taking a lagoon cruise or kayaking through the crystal-clear waters. You'll be surrounded by vibrant marine life and have the opportunity to snorkel or simply relax on one of the secluded islets (motus) that dot the lagoon.

Aitutaki is also steeped in rich cultural traditions and history. The name "Aitutaki" itself means "small but sacred," referring to the island's spiritual significance. Be sure to visit the Taputapuatea marae, an ancient temple complex that was once a central hub for Polynesian voyagers and a sacred site for religious ceremonies.
