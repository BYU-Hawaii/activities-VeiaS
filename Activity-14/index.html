Rarotonga

Discover Rarotonga, the crown jewel of the Cook Islands. Avarua, its vibrant heart, boasts local charm, palm-lined streets, and a bustling Saturday market. Unlike commercialized islands, Raro is an untouched paradise with no big-name stores. Immerse yourself in the laid-back atmosphere, explore the lush interior on tropical hikes, and unwind in Muri's main resort area.
